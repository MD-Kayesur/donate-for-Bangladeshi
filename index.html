Donate for flood at Noakhali, Bangladesh

Rohingya refugees sheltered in Kutupalang Rohingya Camp in Cox's Bazar's Ukhiya have distributed relief among 3,000 families affected by floods in Feni, Noakhali, Lakshmipur and Cumilla.
